Cookies

Cookies are very small text files that are stored on a user's browser. Most cookies have string of characters called a cookie ID, which is associated with the browser on which the cookie was stored. This allows websites to distinguish between cookies of different browsers. Cookies are widely used by websites to provide online services. Cookies are used to keep track of your items in virtual shopping carts when shopping online, remember site preferences on a particular website, or keep you logged into a website so you don't have to log in again later. Cookies are also used to collect data about user activity. This can be analytics purposes, such as tracking how many unique visitors a page receives per month. Or it can be for advertising purposes, in order to personalize ads to users.
